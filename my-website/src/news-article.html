Lands Minister Charges Minerals Development Fund to roll out specific projects in 2023

By MDF Communications

Hon. Samuel A. Jinapor, the Minister for Lands and Natural Resources has tasked the Minerals Development Fund (MDF) Secretariat to undertake a number of development project in mining communities across the country.

The Lands Minister made this disclosure during his engagement with Management and Staff of the Fund on Monday, January 30, 2023 where he emphasized the importance of sustainable development in mining communities.

Key Projects and Initiatives

The Minister outlined several key areas of focus for the MDF in 2023:

  • Infrastructure development in mining communities
  • Educational support programs
  • Healthcare facility improvements
  • Alternative livelihood projects

Community Impact

The planned projects aim to significantly improve the quality of life in mining communities, ensuring that the benefits of mineral resources are felt at the local level.

"The Minerals Development Fund plays a crucial role in ensuring that mining communities benefit directly from the mineral resources extracted from their areas." - Hon. Samuel A. Jinapor

The Minister also stressed the importance of transparency and accountability in the implementation of these projects, ensuring that funds are used effectively for the intended purposes.
