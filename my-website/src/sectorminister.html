Sector minister

The Minerals Development Fund (MDF) was established by the Minerals Development Fund Act, 2016 (Act 912) to provide financial resources to the mining community for sustainable development. The Fund is also mandated to promote the development of the mineral resources of Ghana and to ensure that the benefits of mineral resources accrue to the people of Ghana.

Sector Minister

Hon. Samuel A. Jinapor

Minister for Lands and Natural Resources

Hon. Samuel A. Jinapor is a seasoned Politician and a Barrister at Law. He is a member of the New Patriotic Party (NPP). Hon. Samuel Jinapor was employed in 2012 at the commercial and corporate law firm, Kulendi @ law upon his graduation from the Ghana School of Law. His zeal and enthusiasm at the firm saw him rise from pupil to senior associate.

In 2017, the President of the Republic of Ghana, Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo appointed him as Deputy Chief of Staff in charge of operations at the Office of the President. He is currently the Minister for Lands and Natural Resources and the Member of Parliament for the Damango Constituency of the Savannah Region in Ghana.
